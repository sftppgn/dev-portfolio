My Introduction to Computers and Programming

I liked video games in high school. A lot. I played Starcraft and Everquest for hours at a time. "Do what you love and you'll never work a day in your life", they said. So I purchased a C++ introductory book with my paper route money. I completed basic coding exercises and made reasonable progress for a self-taught person.

I enrolled in a Tech Prep program at the start of my Junior year in high school that earned me college credit. I qualified for a state programming competition through the program, in the C++ programming category. However, C++ wasn't specifically covered in my classes. I was more interested in playing games than creating them with the level of support and motivation I had. I continued to study computer science in high school and college while enjoying other passions such as playing jazz saxophone, running, and personal bodybuilding.
